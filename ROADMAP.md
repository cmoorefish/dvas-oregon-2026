This roadmap outlines the deployment phases of the Digital Verification and Auditing System (DVAS) leading up to the 2026 Oregon Primary.

---

## Phase 1: Technical Hardening (Current)
* **Jan 20, 2026:** Finalize repository documentation (Whitepaper, FAQ, Vision).
* **Jan 30, 2026:** Beta release of the "Candidate Dashboard" for District 52 staff.
* **Feb 15, 2026:** Security stress-test of the Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP) generator.

## Phase 2: Candidate Filing & Onboarding
* **March 3, 2026:** Filing deadline for Elected Incumbents.
* **March 10, 2026:** Final Filing Deadline for all other candidates.
* **March 15, 2026:** "DVAS Setup Week" - Assisting candidates with linking their ORESTAR reporting to the DVAS ledger.

## Phase 3: Live Verification Window
* **April 28, 2026:** Oregon Voter Registration Deadline. (DVAS syncs final voter rolls for citizenship proofs).
* **April 29, 2026:** First day ballots are mailed. DVAS "Transparency Ledger" goes live for public viewing.
* **May 10, 2026:** Preliminary "Foreign Influence Report" generated for state auditors.

## Phase 4: Primary Election & Audit
* **May 19, 2026:** **Election Day.** Real-time monitoring of contribution surges.
* **June 15, 2026:** Final Election Results Certified. 
* **June 30, 2026:** Post-Election Audit Report. Comparing DVAS ledger against final ORESTAR filings for accuracy.

---
*Note: These dates are subject to change based on Secretary of State administrative updates.*
